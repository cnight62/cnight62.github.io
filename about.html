Before this attempt I began my weight loss efforts at 180 pounds and got to 169 through alternate periods of fasting. Then I plateaued at 169 for several months, finally losing another two pounds and reaching 169 pounds. I created a 21 day meal plan with an expected weight loss of 3 pounds and lost, surprise, 3 pounds! However I lost most of the weight in the last week a I started to eat less than the listed portions. Even in week 2 I was still 167 pounds because I kept overeating at night. If I had followed the 21 day meal plan to a T I expect I would have lost 5 pounds. I am including the plan on this website for anyone interested.
